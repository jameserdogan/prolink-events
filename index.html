Why ProLink Events?

Founded in June 2024, ProLink Events was created to solve a simple problem: networking events in Dubai were boring, expensive, and held in stuffy hotel lobbies.

We host our events at the coolest bars and lounges across Dubai, keep prices affordable with free early bird tickets, and focus on creating genuine connections rather than just collecting business cards.

Our attendees don't just show up – they stay long after the event ends because the vibe is that good. That's the ProLink difference.
